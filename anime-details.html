Naruto

Rating: 8.5/10 Genres: Action, Adventure, Fantasy

Naruto Uzumaki is a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja in the village.

Naruto Uzumaki embarks on thrilling adventures filled with action and friendship. His journey is filled with trials that push him towards his dream of becoming the Hokage, making friends and enemies along the way.
